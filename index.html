Our Story

Welcome to Bella Italia, where Italian tradition meets authentic Nepali flavors. Located in the heart of Kathmandu, we bring you the best of both culinary worlds.


Our expert chefs combine traditional Italian recipes with beloved Nepali dishes, using fresh local ingredients and imported Italian specialties to create a unique dining experience.


Whether you're craving classic pasta or traditional dal bhat, we serve every dish with passion and authenticity. Join us for an unforgettable meal!
